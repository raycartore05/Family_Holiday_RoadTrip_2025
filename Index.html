‚≠ê Final Tips for an Amazing Holiday

  1. Leave Early: Beat the crowds and avoid the midday heat - early starts make for better experiences!
  2. Pack Smart: Bring all meals and snacks to save money and time. Include plenty of water!
  3. Book Online: Pre-book Luge, Redwoods, and zoo tickets for potential discounts and skip queues.
  4. Check Weather: New Zealand weather can change quickly - check daily and adjust plans if needed.
  5. Rotate Duties: Share driving responsibilities on long trips to keep everyone fresh.
  6. Plan Breaks: With 10 people (especially kids), build in regular bathroom and stretch breaks.
  7. Take Photos: Capture those special moments - you're making memories that will last a lifetime!
  8. Stay Flexible: Kids can be unpredictable - be ready to adjust timing or skip a stop if needed.
  9. Have Fun: Don't stress over perfect timing - enjoy the journey and time together as a family!
  10. Safety First: Always prioritize safety, especially at beaches, hiking trails, and viewpoints.
